Paper Reviews: Due 11/30/2023&nbsp;
[100 points]

Purpose:
The purpose of this assignment is to teach you how to review and critique
research papers in evolutionary computation.

Task
Choose five of the research papers presented in class and provide a brief review for each of them (no more than one page each). Your review for each paper should include:

\item Paper title [2 points]
\item Which evolutionary computation technique(s) was used in the paper? [4 points]
\item What was the best thing you liked about the paper? [4 points]
\item What was the worst thing about the paper? [4 points]
\item How could you continue/extend the research described in the paper? [6 points]
